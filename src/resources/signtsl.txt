<p>
Signs or classes in the
<a href="https://thotsignlist.org">Thot Sign List</a>
that can be associated with the signs in Unicode.
Created with information from Jorke Grotenhuis.
</p>
<p>
Signs tagged with <b>composition</b> can be formed from other signs by control
characters, and would therefore not be expected to be found in the TSL.
</p>
<p>
Similarly, signs tagged with <b>mirroring</b> or <b>rotation</b> 
can be formed using prospective control characters for mirroring or rotation.
</p>
<p>
Signs tagged with <b>variant</b> are graphical variants of other signs in
Unicode, which may or may not be added to the TSL as class at some point.
</p>
<p>
Code points tagged by <b>typographical</b> do not have an independent meaning
as signs, but are used to form enclosures, and would therefore not be in the
TSL.
</p>
